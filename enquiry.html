Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your account settings > Subscription > Cancel Plan.

Can I listen offline?

Yes, Premium users can download music to play offline on up to 5 devices.

What audio quality do you offer?

We support 96 kbps (Normal), 160 kbps (High), and 320 kbps (Very High) for Premium users.
